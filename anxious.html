Box breathe.

Inhale, Hold, Exhale.

×
  1. Find a comfortable spot.
  2. Take a deep breath in through your nose for 4 seconds.
  3. Hold your breath for 4 seconds.
  4. Exhale through your mouth for 4 seconds.
  5. Pause for 4 seconds.
  6. Repeat this pattern for 4 seconds.

Color Count.

Count your darlings(and your colors).

×

Pick a color(preferrably, your favorite) and count objects in the room that have it. When you run of objects to count, pick another color.

Cool down and ice dive.

Chill Out.

×
  1. Fill a large bowl with cold water and ice cubes.
  2. Submerge your face for 10 seconds.
  3. If you don't prefer to submerge your face into water, splash your face with cold water, or take a cold shower.
